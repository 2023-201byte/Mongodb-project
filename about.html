About Crime Reporting System

Learn more about our mission, vision, and services

About Us

The Crime Reporting System (CRS) is a digital platform designed to empower citizens to report crimes efficiently and safely. Our system ensures that every complaint is recorded, monitored, and addressed by the relevant authorities in a timely manner.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the criminal justice process accessible, transparent, and secure for all members of society. We aim to reduce delays, improve reporting efficiency, and provide a confidential channel for sensitive cases.

Our Vision

To be a trusted digital interface between citizens and law enforcement agencies, promoting safety, justice, and accountability in the community.

What We Offer

  • Online FIR registration from anywhere.
  • Anonymous tips submission for secure reporting.
  • Women Help Desk for confidential assistance and protection.
  • Track the status of your complaint using CNIC and FIR copy.
  • Access to relevant crime-related news and media updates.

Why Choose CRS

Our system is user-friendly, secure, and designed with the citizen's convenience in mind. By digitizing crime reporting, we help create a safer community where every complaint matters and timely action is taken.
